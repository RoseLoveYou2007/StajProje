Mori Calliope is an English Virtual YouTuber associated with hololive, as part of its English (EN) branch first generation of VTubers alongside Ninomae Ina'nis, Takanashi Kiara, Watson Amelia, and Gawr Gura.

Personality

Calliope is equally excited by both rapping and "reaping". Her favorite weapons include her scythe (nicknamed "Ricky") and the flail, a medieval weapon made from a spiked metal ball on a chain. Although fluent in English, Calliope often fails to understand current modes of speech, such as the use of contractions or modern slang. She is easily flustered by romance, leading some to call her the "tsundereaper".
She was introduced simultaneously on both hololive's and hololive's English Twitter accounts and on hololive English's YouTube channel in a small video with the rest of the first generation on 8 September 2020. Her Twitter account was created on July, but only made her first tweet on her official introduction on 8 September. Her YouTube channel was created on 16 July but she debuted on 12 September also releasing her first original song: "失礼しますが、RIP♡" or known in English as "Excuse My Rudeness, But Could You Please RIP?" a rap song written by herself.

2020

On 22 October, Calli reached 500,000 YouTube subscribers.
On 1 November, she released a new original song "Cursed Night"
On 14 December, she reached 800,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel, Calli celebrated with an unarchived karaoke stream.
On 29 December, she made a singing appearance in Tsunomaki Watame's second original song "Douten Hitsuji".

2021

On 7 January, she reached 900,000 YouTube subscribers.
On 25 January, Calli reached 1,000,000 YouTube subscribers during a subscriber endurance stream, making her the 7th hololive member to reach that milestone and the 2nd to do so on the English branch after Gawr Gura.
On 31 January, Calli debuted her eighth original song "Off With Their Heads"
On 15 February, she announced during a stream that as part of her birthday celebration she would release a second EP containing 5 songs, 4 of them original. Calli also announced that this EP would be released including physical copies.
On 4 March, Calli debuted her ninth original song "Ibasho" (Where I Belong) as part of a collaboration with the game Princess Connect! Re:Dive, during a stream on her YouTube channel.
On 25 March, she announced that she collaborated with Rikka from Holostars for an original song called "Spiral Tones", an MV is in the making.
On 1 April, the holomyth members debuts their new chibi models.
On 30 April, hololive announced that the holomyth members will be getting their new costumes starting from the 3rd of May. Calli's new costume will be on the 3rd of May.
